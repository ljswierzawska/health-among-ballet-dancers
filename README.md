# Can Ballet Ever Be Healthy?

A data story exploring the physical and mental health toll on professional ballet dancers.

## About

This story draws on an interview with Dorota Nowak, a professional dancer and choreographer, who shared her experience on the attitudes towards mental health among ballet dancers. The data visualizations present findings from two studies about the subject. The purpose is to ask whether the dance world is finally catching up with contemporary wellness standards or whether it remains rooted in a culture of relentless endurance.

## Data Sources

- **Junge, A. & Hauschild, A. (2023).** "Behind the Curtain: Prevalence of Symptoms of Depression, Generalised Anxiety and Eating Disorders in 147 Professional Dancers." *Sports Medicine – Open.* [doi.org/10.1186/s40798-023-00638-9](https://doi.org/10.1186/s40798-023-00638-9)

- **Junge, A. et al. (2024).** "Health Problems of Professional Ballet Dancers: an Analysis of 1,627 Weekly Self-Reports." *Sports Medicine – Open.* [doi.org/10.1186/s40798-024-00753-1](https://doi.org/10.1186/s40798-024-00753-1)
